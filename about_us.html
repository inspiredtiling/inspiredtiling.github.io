About Us

Inspired Tiling has been around for a number of years now and during that time we've tiled countless kitchens and bathrooms. My name is Nigel Singleton and my job is to ensure that when I leave your home, you have a tiled bathroom, kitchen, dining or living area that you can be proud of.

The services we offer are more than just tiling and they include installation of underfloor heating, plumbing and plastering. Take a look at a list of our services below. If you don't see what you require in that list then please do give me a call on 0786 222 5108 to discuss and we will see what can be done.

We are based in the Watford area and cover Watford and many of the surrounding areas including Bushey, Rickmansworth, NW London, Northwood, Pinner and Harrow.
